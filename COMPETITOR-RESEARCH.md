# Research: what top fitness & trading coaching sites do (July 2026)

Summary of what drives revenue on the leading platforms, and how SARDAARJI now compares.

## What the market charges
- **Online fitness coaching:** entry $100–175/mo, mid $150–350/mo, high-touch/1-on-1 $350–800+/mo.
  Solo-coach software itself runs $20–150/mo (FitBudd ~$149/mo, Kajabi ~$179/mo).
- **Trading education:** memberships commonly $47–99/mo; high-ticket courses $797 → $2,997 for tiered
  content libraries (18h → 200h+). Community is usually sold as an *upgrade*, not in the base tier.

**Takeaway:** our $19–199/mo tiers are well-placed for a starting brand. Room to add a high-ticket
"course" or "1-on-1 intensive" later ($500–2,000) — that's where big revenue jumps come from.

## The honest path to $10K/month
$10K/mo ≈ **~200 members at ~$50 avg**, or fewer members with high-ticket coaching. It comes from three
dials, in order:
1. **Traffic** — the free tool, free guide, SEO, and content (blog, YouTube, Instagram) bring visitors.
2. **Conversion** — the trial, guarantee, FAQ, founding offer (all now in place) turn visitors into trials.
3. **Retention** — the journal, plans, streaks and community keep them paying month after month.

The product is now built to convert. The remaining growth work is **payments on**, **traffic in**, and
**consistent content** — none of which a website alone can do; they need you (or ad/marketing spend).
