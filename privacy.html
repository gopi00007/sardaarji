Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. What we collect

  • Account info — your name, email, and login (via email or Google sign-in).
  • Data you enter — trades, workouts, fitness details, profile, and community posts.
  • Email sign-ups — if you request our free guide, we store your email to send it and occasional updates.

2. How we use it

To provide the service (save your data, generate plans, run the community), to communicate with you, and to improve the Platform. We do not sell your personal data.

3. Where it's stored

Your data is stored securely with our infrastructure provider (Supabase). Access is restricted so that your private data (trades, workouts, fitness details) is visible only to your account. Community profiles and posts you create are visible to other members by design.

4. Third parties

We use trusted providers for hosting, authentication, and (when enabled) payments and email delivery. These providers process data only to deliver their service to us.

5. Your rights

You can access, correct, or delete your data at any time. To delete your account and associated data, contact us and we'll process your request.

6. Cookies

We use only essential cookies/local storage needed to keep you signed in and remember your preferences.

7. Contact

Privacy questions? Email hello@fitnessandtradingcoach.netlify.app.
